What Freshmans Say About the Course?

According to the freshmen, they appreciate the minimal effort required in the English 100 course. The professors are easily approachable and try to create a positive learning environment. There is a great emphasis on writing practice. Prompt grading provides timely feedback for continuous improvement. Clear learning objectives and the instructor's ability to stimulate interest enhance the overall positive experience for students.

Faculty

Dr. Morris Young
Director
Dr. Morris Young expertise lies in Composition and Rhetoric, notably in Race and Rhetoric, Spatial Rhetorics, and Literacy Studies. They are dedicated to teaching writing, emphasizing literacies and identities, and exhibit a keen interest in Asian American Literature and Culture.
Dr. Sara Kelm
Associate Director
Dr. Sara Kelm interests span Composition pedagogy, audio/multimodal composition, and andragogy, with a focus on the intersections between creative nonfiction/memoir and women's rhetorics. Their scholarly pursuits also extend to popular culture studies and religious rhetorics.
